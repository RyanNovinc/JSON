1. Introduction

AppLabs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the AI Workout Generator mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

By using AI Workout Generator, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our Service.

2. Data Storage and Collection

AI Workout Generator is designed with privacy in mind. We prioritize local data storage and minimize server-side data collection.

Information We Collect:

  • Workout Data: Exercise routines, workout logs, progress tracking, and training statistics stored locally on your device
  • Account Information: Email address and basic profile information for account creation and support
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app usage patterns to improve functionality (collected via privacy-focused analytics)
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase data managed through RevenueCat for subscription management
  • AI Integration Data: Temporary processing of workout program imports (not stored permanently)
  • Feedback Data: Optional feedback submissions to improve AI-generated workout quality

Local Storage Priority:

Your workout routines, exercise logs, and personal fitness data are stored locally on your device. This ensures your sensitive fitness information remains under your control and is not transmitted to external servers unless explicitly required for specific features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the AI Workout Generator service
  • Processing workout program imports and validating exercise data
  • Managing your subscription through RevenueCat integration
  • Sending important service notifications and updates
  • Improving app functionality based on anonymous usage patterns
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Enhancing AI workout generation algorithms through optional feedback
  • Ensuring app security and preventing misuse

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers:

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. AI Integration and External Services

AI Workout Generator integrates with AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) through user-initiated actions:
